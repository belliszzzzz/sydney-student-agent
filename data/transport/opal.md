# Opal Card Basics (Sydney)

Opal card is a smart travel card used for public transport in Sydney and across NSW.

## How to get an Opal card
- Buy from convenience stores or newsagents with Opal signs.
- Buy at some train station counters.
- Order online via Transport for NSW website.

## How to top up
- Online top up using Opal website or Opal Travel App.
- Auto top up can be enabled.
- Top up at participating retail stores.

## Where Opal can be used
- Trains
- Buses
- Ferries
- Light Rail

## Important usage rules
- Tap on when entering.
- Tap off when exiting trains, ferries, and light rail.
